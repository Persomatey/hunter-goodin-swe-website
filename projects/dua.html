Disney's Uncharted Adventure
(WayForward Technologies)
Buttons:

An adventure on Disney's Wish cruise ship where players are guided around the ship completing tasks and playing games on their phones. Innovative technology, like augmented reality and physical effects, create a hidden layer of magic. A digital menu board might showcase what's for lunch in the afternoon, but later it will host a cooking lesson in Tiana's own kitchen.

A split between Unity Engine written in C# (running using 'Unity as a Library' within a proprietary app) and Phaser 3 written in Typescript/Node.JS (for all digital signage). I primarily worked as the lead developer on all Augmented Reality (AR) experiences as well as Prop experiences with physical props that react in real time to your actions in the game on your phone. I engineered all of the peer-to-peer and peer-to-server multiplayer functionality for those games. I also did a lot of work on the Digital Signage games in Phaser, taking the lead on several games.
