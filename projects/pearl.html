Digital Hazard | Nvidia Internal Game Jam

A "Tricky Towers"-like stacking game for an internal game jam within Nvidia, revolving around incorporating a variety of Nvidia technologies into a single prototype game.

I did all of the Unity development in C# and incorporated Nvidia's RTX tech stack, including: DLSS, PhysX, Ray Traced Lighting, Ray Traced Ambient Occlusion (RTAO), Light Cluster, Ray Traced Reflection (RTR), Ray Traced Global Illumination (RTGI), Ray Traced Shadows, and Path Traced Lights.
