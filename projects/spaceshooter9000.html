Space Shooter 9000
(COC CSC Fall 2017)
Buttons:

A side-scrolling space shooter arcade game with a low poly aesthetic. Battle through wave after wave of enemy ships as you plunge deep into enemy territory.

Made in the Unity Engine written in C#. My first game as President of the Computer Science Club, we went for a simple arcade shooter. I operated as director and lead programmer for this project. I worked on nearly every aspect of the game. I programmed most of the enemy AI, the player projectiles, the enemy spawn system, the point and high score system, the health system, the unlock system, 3D modeling and animation, and many other aspects of the game. You can see my name commented on nearly every script in the game.
