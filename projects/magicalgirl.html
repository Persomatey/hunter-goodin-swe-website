Saffia is a magical girl in her late 20s. Balancing her full time human job with her full time magical girl job has her working from morning to night! At this point, she’s deeply regretting signing that mysterious contract a cuddly, flying creature offered her. Faced with bills, a full time job, and creatures from the spirit realm, how does a girl find time to relax?! One day Saffia’s magically miserable life is thrown into a spiral. She’s faced with two new eccentric coworkers, magical girls looking for a team up, and the mysterious new villain: Midnight, all in the same week! Will Saffia join these new magical girls and rediscover magic in her adult life, or continue down her path of cynicism from 9 to 5?

Made for the Magical Girl Game Jam #11. I worked on the UI programming for the game using RenPy.
