Funem
(GMTK 2021 "Joined Together" Game Jam)
Buttons:

A 2D top-down Zelda-like game where you play as two characters, Wasd and Arro making their way through a puzzle dungeon. Only caveat, Wasd and Arro are joined together by a rope! Fight your way through rats, puzzle your way through rooms, and use spacial reasoning to beat every level.

Made in the Unity Engine written in C# during the GMTK Game Jam 2021 with the theme "Joined Together" in just 2 days. I solo developed the game, including doing all of the programming for the game as well as the UI/UX design, SFX, general game design and level design, and some of the game's Art.
