Draconic Defenders
(Mini Jam 151 "Dragons" Game Jam)
Buttons:

A top-down tower defense game, where you're hatching dragons to defend against a waves of dwarves!

Made in the Unity Engine written in C# during the Mini Jam "Dragons" Game Jam with the theme "Dragons" and the limitation "Endless Greed" in just 3 days. I worked with another developer on the game, acting as the game's director and lead programmer. I did most of the programming for the game as well as the UI/UX design, SFX, general game design.

(5th place in "Enjoyment")
(11th place in "Concept")
(15th place overall)
