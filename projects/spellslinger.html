Spellslinger
(Mini Jam 150 "Magic" Game Jam)
Buttons:

A first person magical shooter. The world is dark, so use your magic to light that way. Also use magic to attack enemies. Each enemy has a different element. Carefully select which spell ammo you're loading into your magazine before attacking. Play through the short tutorial to learn the ropes, then hop into arena mode to fight off waves of enemies!

Made in the Unreal Engine 5.3 written in C++ and Blueprints during the Mini Jam "Magic" Game Jam with the theme "Magic" and the limitation "Light is the Way" in just 3 days. I solo developed the game, including doing all of the programming for the game as well as the UI/UX design, SFX, general game design.

(25th place in "Concept")
(32nd place in "Enjoyment")
